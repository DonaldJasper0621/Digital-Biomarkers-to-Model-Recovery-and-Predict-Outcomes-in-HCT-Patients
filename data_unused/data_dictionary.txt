## Data Processing Notes

1. All timestamps are standardized to 24-hour format
2. All dates are relative to transplant date (DaysFromTransplant)
3. Personal identifiers are replaced with anonymous IDs
4. Heart rate values are filtered to physiologically plausible range (30-200 bpm)
5. Sleep duration is limited to plausible range (2-24 hours)
6. Mood scores are limited to scale range (1-10)
7. All time-based variables are measured in days from transplant date
8. Day 0 = Transplant date
9. Positive values indicate days after transplant
10. Maximum follow-up period is 100 days for clinical outcomes

## PROMIS T-Score Interpretation

- Mean score = 50
- Standard deviation = 10
- Higher scores indicate more of the concept being measured
- For symptom measures (anxiety, depression, fatigue, pain interference, sleep disturbance):
  - Higher scores = More symptoms/worse health
- For function measures (physical function, cognitive function, participation):
  - Higher scores = Better function/health

## Censoring

The following information is removed during censoring:
- Original participant IDs
- Original device IDs
- Exact dates
- Location information
- Any other potentially identifying information

## Missing Data Codes

- "OHSU patient": Missing hospital days data for OHSU patients
- A blank cell indicates missing or not applicable data

## Coverage and Time Periods

- Full day coverage: 1440 minutes (24 hours)
- Morning: 6:00-12:00 (360 minutes)
- Afternoon: 12:00-18:00 (360 minutes)
- Evening: 18:00-24:00 (360 minutes)
- Night: 00:00-6:00 (360 minutes)

## Time Coverage Calculation

time_coverage = n_minutes / 1440 # where 1440 is minutes in a day

## Data Quality Metrics

- Valid coverage: time_coverage ≥ 0.8 (80% of day)
- Valid heart rate range: 30-200 bpm
- Valid step counts: ≥ 0 steps per minute
- Total daily activities: should sum to ≤ 1440 minutes
